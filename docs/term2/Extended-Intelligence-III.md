## ★   Extended Intelligence III
</div>
<p style="font-size:15px; line-height:1.5; text-align:center; max-width:700px; margin: 0 auto 40px auto;">
My exploration of a new reality for human and machine co existence landed in the realm of the dreamscape. Dreams are understood as a primary way to access the unconcious, a container of fantasies & memories banished & repressed from conciousness. 
</p>
 </div>

</div>
<p style="font-size:15px; line-height:1.5; text-align:center; max-width:700px; margin: 0 auto 40px auto;">
In order to exteneralize the dreamscape and create a feeling of sharing your cognition with a system that can learn and adapt to your presents and outputs, I proposed a brain organoid that would will bring a person to a state of REM sleep where they lucid dream. 
</p>
 </div>
</div>
